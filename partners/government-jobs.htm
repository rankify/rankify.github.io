1. Job Opportunities with the City of Vancouver
The City of Vancouver is a large municipality employing approximately 9,000 people in various locations in the City. Applications are accepted ONLY for current vacancies advertised on this page and on our 24-hour Job Line at (604)873.7264. * Only those applicants who are legally entitled to work in Canada can be considered for these advertised positions. Visit the Canadian Citizenship and Immigration web site at Link to external website http://www.cic.gc.ca for further information. * Information on how and where to apply for jobs can be found in the Employment Information Brochure on this web site.

Job Opportunities: The District accepts applications for current positions only. These positions are listed at the bottom of this page. Postings that have recently closed are listed under the archived articles. Applications are not accepted for archived postings.
