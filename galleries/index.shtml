1. Golden Cactus Gallery - Vancouver
Since their first visit to los cabos in 1991, Chris MacClure and his partner Marilyn Hurst have considered the Baja a paradise on earth and eventually in 1997 they decided to make Cabo home. The Gallery began as a studio for Chris but its evolution over the past 9 years has been as remarkable as the growth of Cabo itself. Since its inception, the 2nd storey gallery has been a beacon to other artists and its success a testimony to the spirit of art.
2. Kurbatoff Art Gallery, Vancouver B.C.
Dynamic and open-minded, the Gallery is focused on the promotion of contemporary Canadian artists, from emerging and mid-career to well-established. The most important criterion of the selection is a high level of mastery combined with distinctive vision which brings thought and soul in a work of art and makes it truly original and unique.
