VanStart Sponsors & Link Partners


1. Vancouver Painters Meet - Vancouver B.C.
Painters Unite! Meet other artists in your area. The brush and canvas do not have to be your only friends! This group will meet in the Vancouver (BC) area on a monthly basis. Locations will be chosen in advance and posted to the group for an RSVP. Come talk about art, galleries, your work, or whatever!
2. Gregg Simpson CyberGallery
The gallery features the work of west coast Canadian artist, Gregg Simpson. The site features works from the 1960s to the present in the following mediums: painting, drawing, mixed media, photography and digital art. Simpsons work is featured in public and private collections in Canada, the US, Europe, Asia and South America.
3. Blue Lotus Arts, Interior Glass Elements - Vancouver B.C
Artist Nicala Swiednicki creates interior glass elements for residential and commercial spaces, designs her own contemporary glass expressions, captures poses in glass for Yoga & Pilates studios, creates extraordinary glass portrait accents for photographers and their clients, as well as, teaches communities the intricate art of mosaic.
4. Cedar Moon Glass Studio & Gallery
Cedar Moon Glass Studio is located near Vancouver, BC, Canada producing works in glass using the processes of sandcarving and kilnforming .
